    ```

    **3. What it does**
    Short prose paragraph (3–5 sentences): On every prompt you submit to Claude Code, pls-fix checks whether it's a fix/debug/code request. If eligible, it scores your desperation level (0–9+ points) across 10 signals — repeated attempts, ALL CAPS, deadline words, late-night timestamps, etc. — and maps the score to one of 5 tiers. The tier phrase is appended as context before Claude sees your prompt. At session end, a box-drawing report shows your stats.
